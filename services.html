Training

We will cover all the immediate issues you have with your dog, whether it be jumping up, pulling on the leash, barking, biting, chewing your things, etc.. We will set up a plan on how to work with your issues so that your dog can clearly understand what you are asking of them. Clear communication is key for teaching your dog to succeed!

Daycare

Day School is a teaching tool I use to help your dog learn respectful social skills while interacting with other dogs. They get one mandatory nap time per day, a “place” session (to teach patience), a treadmill session (to build self-confidence), as well as multiple free social times with all the other dogs.

Book Now

Boarding

When your dog boards with me they are taken out with the Day School dogs and get to work on their social skill’s multiple times every day. They get a couple of hours of mandatory downtime for napping between social sessions with the Day School dogs. Each dog will sleep in individual kennels I set up for them.
